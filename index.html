About Me

I am currently a 4th year UCSC student majoring in Art and Design: Games and Playable Media. I have experience in design software including Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, ClipStudioPro, and Blender, and experience with Python, Javascript, HTML/CSS, and JQuery. I mainly focus on design and have helped make several physical games, but I'm working towards improving my digital art as well. Recently I've been trying to improve my pixel art in CSP and 3D art in Blender.


My Work

Career Path Podcasts

UI/UX Design | Figma

10 week long school project where we designed an app from scratch in Figma geared towards helping highschoolers consider options for their future. After interviewing highschool students and teachers, we decided to make an app that would contain a database of podcasts and vlogs where students could find careers that better suit their interests.

My contributions for this project included designing the poster detailing our process from ideation to the final interactive prototype which we used to explain our idea during a project fair. On the interactive prototype, I also helped design the pages for the search engine, downloads, uploads, and favorites.

Frankenstein's Monster

Text-Based Game Design | Twine

Text-based game where one plays as a monster being shunned from society. You can take different paths in the game that affect your ending, but there is no happy ending.

This was a solo project I worked on during a 3 week long game jam with the prompt "Monster Mash." I used this project to experiment with using darker tones and themes in games because I usually go more optimistic and bright with my game aesthetics.
